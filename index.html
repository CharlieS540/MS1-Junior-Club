About Junior Club

About Us

Junior Club was created to provide a fun a safe enviroment for young skiers and snowboarders to improve their skills and make new friends. The children in the junior groups have an oportunity to try many different activities including slalom, boxes, rails, waves, kicker and much more whilst supervised by qualified instructors and coaches. Our coaches have many years of experience and strive to give every participant the help needed to progress in their chosen sport and make every session as fun as possible.

How to Join

Skiers

To join the Junior Club as a skier your child will need to be able to control their speed at all times, stop when asked, link turns in snow plow and use the poma lift. If you are happy they are comfortable with all the above please attend the junior assessment on Saturday from 1pm to 1:30pm.

Snowboarders

To join the Junior Club as a snowboarder your child will need to be able to side slip on both their toe and heel edge, control their speed and stop when asked. If you are happy they are comfortable with all the above please attend the junior assessment on Monday from 5pm to 5:30pm.

Session Information

Skiers

Junior Club for Skiers is on Saturday between 9am and 1pm. The session costs £10 per 90 minutes and all equipment is included. All participants must wear a helmet, long sleeve top or coat, trousers or salopettes and gloves.

Snowboarders

Junior Club for Snowboarders is on Monday from 5:30pm to 7pm. The session costs £10 and all equipment is included. All participants must wear a helmet, long sleeve top or coat, trousers or salopettes and gloves.
